Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The vision for Bangladesh 2.0 centers on a prosperous, inclusive, and sustainable nation. It envisions eradicating poverty, fostering innovation, and empowering youth through education and entrepreneurship. With a focus on climate resilience, social equity, and technological advancement, Bangladesh 2.0 aspires to be a global model of progress and sustainability.The vision for Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on prosperity, inclusion, and sustainability, aiming to eliminate poverty, foster innovation, and empower youth through education and entrepreneurship. Emphasizing climate resilience, green energy, and equitable opportunities, it envisions a technologically advanced, globally competitive nation driven by creativity, social equity, and sustainable development practices.
